Intecracy

Meeting in the middle

The Problem

The world has moved from monarchy and autocracy towards democracy. But none of these systems represent stable growth for everyone. Whichever we pick, there will be issues.

We could try Technocracy, where technical experts make decisions for all of us. But how can we ensure those decisions are good all the time? We can't.

Something New

We need something universal. Something that covers every human being's basic needs first, making sure no one is excluded:

  • Shelter
  • Clothing
  • Healthy Food
  • Commute
  • Communication

The rest are less critical to survival. But to make this work, we need two additional factors:

1. Integrity

Give back to society. Not because you have to, but because you're part of it.

2. Universal Education

For all types of personalities. Not everyone learns the same way. Not everyone thinks the same way.

What This Changes

If you don't have to worry about your basic needs, you no longer have to worry about your life. Now you can choose: want little and do little, or want more and have the means to achieve it.

Have a community that helps you, because nothing is holding you back. Have experts you can question. Solve problems together.

Without integrity and universal education, we'll always have problems with basic needs. There will always be greed.

Meeting in the Middle

That's what makes sense to me. Not left, not right. Not pure democracy, not technocracy. Something that combines the best of what we know with integrity at its core.

That's why I'm making this word, "Intecracy", public domain. Let's share it with others.

Piotr Ficner
